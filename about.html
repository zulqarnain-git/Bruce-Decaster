About Bruce's

Global HR Executive & Nonfiction Author

Leading Strategies Across
Continents & Cultures

Bruce Decaster is a senior global HR executive with more than two decades of experience leading people strategies across the Americas, Europe, and Africa. Known for his ability to navigate complex cultures, elevate talent, and drive organizational transformation, Bruce has guided multinational companies through rapid growth, restructuring, and cultural change.

Today, he channels this global experience into his work as an emerging nonfiction author—writing with a clear mission: to make the workplace more human, more equitable, and more honest about the invisible forces that shape employee experience.

Across his corporate leadership and writing, Bruce exposes hidden systems, elevates marginalized perspectives, and equips leaders with tangible tools to build healthier, more inclusive organizations. Whether conducting regional HR audits, coaching senior executives, or designing leadership frameworks used across multinational teams, Bruce brings a rare combination of global insight, operational rigor, and human-centered leadership.

Beyond his corporate work, he is a passionate advocate for learning, cultural intelligence, and gender equity in the workplace—values that deeply inform all of his writing and thought leadership.
